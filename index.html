Abstract

The abstract track supports original scientific work in agricultural biotechnology, environmental health, climate change, food systems, digital innovation and related disciplines. Contributions should be concise, well argued and methodologically sound.

Accepted submissions may be shown in oral, poster or digital presentation format, depending on the final session allocation. The language and tone of all material should remain formal, precise and publication ready.

Submission areas

Genome tools, molecular breeding, diagnostics, biosystems and related research.
Adaptation, resilience, biodiversity, pollution, ecosystem services and sustainability.
Governance, digital tools, innovation ecosystems, start-up pathways and public policy.
