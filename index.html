Abstract

Abstracts are welcomed from natural sciences, biotechnology, climate adaptation, sustainability studies, digital systems and related research areas.

Submissions should present a clear question, a sound method and a concise result or expected contribution. The tone should remain professional and publication ready, with enough space for scientific depth.

Submission themes

Genome tools, molecular breeding, diagnostics, biosystems and translational science.
Adaptation, resilience, biodiversity, ecosystem services, environmental health and sustainability.
Governance, regulation, digital tools, startup pathways and evidence-based policy.
